Welcome to Likii Hill School

Located in Nanyuki, Likii Hill School is a private, school in Nanyuki, Kenya, and a pioneer of exceptional modern education as the Premier School in Laikipia County. Students aged 1 to 18 gain unrivalled independent day education across two exquisite sites in the leafy suburbs of Nanyuki, with premium boarding school provision extended to the main campus. At Likii Hill School, children achieve academic excellence with ly-recognised courses and complementary activities to develop their talents.

Every child is inherently unique. Therefore, at our school in Nanyuki, Kenya, our experienced educators apply a personalised teaching approach to meet the needs of each individual. The effectiveness of our child-centred methodology is outstanding. Likii Hill students achieve the best KCPE and KCSE results in Laikipia County for over a decade, and embody a strong value system that unlocks their full potential, preparing them for lifelong success.

Our Schools in Nanyuki

Likii Hill School proudly educates our bright, young learners with a 8-4-4 & CBC Curriculum at two expansive sites in the charming regions of Runda and Karen in the heart of Nanyuki. Premium boarding is available at our main campus.

Why Choose Our School in Nanyuki, Kenya?

Likii Hill School provides a character-focused, values-driven education through a school-wide Global Citizenship programme.

As a Round Square school, Likii Hill promotes experiential learning opportunities for all students through local, regional, and experiences and excursions.

Expanding the Bounds of Learning with Virtual Reality

Our classrooms are no longer static entities confined by four walls. An Inspired School

As part of the Inspired Group, Likii Hill School is committed to providing a modern, forward-looking education while remaining anchored in the culture and traditions of Nanyuki and actively participating in the local community.

While attending an Inspired School, Likii Hill’s local and students benefit from the following:

Academic, Cultural and Social Exchanges

Academic, Cultural and Social Exchanges

Inspired enables our learning and teaching community to immerse themselves in diverse learning environments, educational systems and skills.

Exchange Programmes

Exchange Programmes

Inspired offers exciting opportunities for travel to other countries and work alongside peers attending other schools in the Group. Students form beneficial networks and build lifelong friendships while developing their social, language and academic skills by broadening their perspectives.
